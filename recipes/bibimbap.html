Bibimbap Recipe

Bibimbap is a delicious Korean dish that consists of mixed veggies and beef alongside a sweet but savory bibimbap sauce.

Ingredients

  • Minced Beef
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Brown Sugar
  • Assorted Vegetables
  • Steamed Rice
  • Eggs
  • Gochujang
  • Apple Vinegar
  • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Cut veggies
  2. Mix the sauce with gochujang, sugar, sesame oil, and water
  3. Cook the rice, beef, and the eggs separately
  4. Plate them all together
  5. Mix them well and enjoy!
