PIzza Recipe

Pizza is a delicious Italian dish that consists of dough, tomato sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Minced Beef
  • Olive Oil
  • Assorted Vegetables
  • Dough
  • Bacon
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Cut veggies
  2. Make the dough
  3. Put sauce, cheese, and toppings on dough
  4. Bake the pizza in the oven
  5. Cut them into 8 equal parts and enjoy!
